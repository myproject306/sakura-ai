Sakura AI is free to use. This policy covers any future premium features or paid services.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Free Platform

Sakura AI is currently a free platform. All AI tools, templates, and features are available at no cost to registered users. Since the platform is free, there are no charges and therefore no refunds applicable for standard use.

2. Future Premium Services

If Sakura AI introduces premium features, paid plans, or additional services in the future, this Refund Policy will apply to those purchases. We will update this policy and notify users before any paid features are introduced.

3. General Refund Principles

For any future paid services, our refund policy will be based on the following principles:

  • 7-Day Refund Window: Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • Minimal Usage Requirement: Refunds will be considered if no significant usage of the paid features has occurred.
  • Good Faith: We evaluate all refund requests in good faith and aim to resolve them fairly.
  • One-Time Policy: Refunds are generally available once per customer account.

4. Eligible Refund Situations

Refunds may be granted in the following situations:

  • Technical issues that prevent access to paid features, not resolved within 48 hours.
  • Accidental duplicate purchases.
  • Significant discrepancy between advertised features and actual service delivery.
  • Unauthorized charges on your account (subject to investigation).

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will generally not be granted in the following situations:

  • Requests made after the 7-day refund window has passed.
  • Significant usage of the paid service has already occurred.
  • Change of mind after extensive use of the platform.
  • Violation of our Terms & Conditions leading to account suspension.
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated content quality (as AI outputs vary by nature).

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for any paid service:

  • Contact our support team at support@sakura-ai.com within the eligible timeframe.
  • Include your account email, order/transaction ID, and reason for the refund request.
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days.
  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

7. Chargebacks

We encourage users to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with their bank or payment provider. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension. We are committed to resolving all legitimate disputes fairly and promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
